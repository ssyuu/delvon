    TOP 5 BEST ISLAND IN MALAYSIA



    RAWA ISLAND

    Rawa Island is a small island, located in Johor and 16 km from Mersing. What is interesting about this island, the island is very awake and peaceful, the sea water is clear blue, the sand is white and makes this island a major attraction in Johor. Surrounded by a blue ocean rich in marine life and colorful corals and lush forests for you to explore, and best for in idyllic tropical island is a haven for those pursuit of peace and wishing to spend time with their family and things to do is snorkeling, volleyball beach, and water slide, best time to visit is March-September



    MATAKING ISLAND

    Mataking Island which is located about 45 minutes from Sipadan Island is also often a holiday destination among Europeans for honeymooning because of its beautiful resort facing the sea. As on most other islands in Sabah, Mataking Island also has a diving area to see marine life and coral reefs and the most interesting is that you have the opportunity to meet shark whales, best things to do is you can snorkeling, water wolrd, wedding, and diving, the best time to go is April-September



    SEMBILAN ISLAND

    Pulau Sembilan is the one that glows when night falls which is called "Blue Tear" or "Blue Sand". This phenomenon is the result of numerous marine organisms in the coastal areas of Pulau Sembilan. The name of this luminous organism is Ostracod Crustaceans or Phytoplankton. Activity that we can do in Sembilan Island is blue tears and sky mirror and best time for you to visit is February-October



    TIOMAN ISLAND

    Tioman Island is a teardrop -shaped island located 58 kilometers off the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia, in the South China Sea. Tioman Island is the largest island in the group of 64 existing volcanic islands and its prominent profile with two peaks of Mount Simukut offers a prominent landmark to this beautiful island, reputed as one of the ten most beautiful and charming islands in the world. The most popular activities that you can do in this island is hiking, water fall, golf, marin playground and learn to diving. The best time you can visit is February-October



    POM POM ISLAND

    The bottom of the waters is said to be quite rich in marine life of unique species. Based on the study, the number of fish species found here is more than 220 in total, while coral reefs are more than 250 species. Not only that, the island is also a location for turtles to land and hatch their eggs from May to September. The island located only 30 km from Semporna Town is not complete with other famous islands, and there is only one accommodation offered here. The most value activities that you can do is cycling, hatching or incubation of turtle eggs, ocean kayaking and the best time you can visit from January-September
